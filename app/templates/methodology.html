How we source a brief

  • Vendor release notes, changelogs, docs, or policy statements come first.
  • We summarize the builder impact in terms of capability, cost, latency, reliability, safety, licensing, or compliance.
  • We avoid summaries that outrun the available evidence.
  • We keep source links visible so readers can verify the original claim and contract language.
